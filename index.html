What is Quantum Optimization?

As the industry waits for scalable fault-tolerant quantum computers, many quantum-inspired solutions can offer benefits today, most notably in optimization. Quantum optimization has focused mostly on quantum annealing, which can be used to solve QUBOs. A QUBO is a mathematical formulation used to represent optimization problems as a quadratic objective function involving binary variables. What is QUBO?

QUBO, which stands for Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization, is a combinatorial optimization problem with a wide variety of applications ranging from finance to machine learning. BEIT’s QUBO solver is a (quantum-inspired) classical piece of software that revolutionizes problem-solving in its ability to address some NP-complete problems.
